TOKiMONSTA

Rainforest Alliance

Nature has always had a huge influence on my art. I really try to translate the feeling I get from being outside as best as I sonically can through my music. In addition to being inspired by nature itself, I’m extremely passionate about environmental issues. I wanted to align the release of “Gamble” with an organization that puts just as much focus on forest sustainability as it does the communities who help them thrive. - Jennifer Lee, TOKiMONSTA

 TOKiMONSTA - Gamble

Jennifer Lee, better known as TOKiMONSTA, is a Los Angeles native who is known for her unique take on indie electronic/r&b/dance music. Trained in piano as a child, but brought up on a strict diet of the West Coast rap that dominated LA radio stations, TOKiMONSTA was inspired from an early age to explore music production.

TOKiMONSTA chose to direct the proceeds of her newest single “Gamble” to the Rainforest Alliance. Started in 1987, the Rainforest Alliance has worked to build a global alliance of people who share a vision of strong forests and thriving communities. Today, they work with rural communities in 78 countries around the world to conserve forests, support sustainable livelihoods, and restore balance to the planet we call home.
