Nature has always had a huge influence on my art. I really try to translate the feeling I get from being outside as best as I sonically can through my music. I wanted to align the release of “Gamble” with an organization that puts just as much focus on forest sustainability as it does the communities who help them thrive. - Jennifer Lee, TOKiMONSTA
